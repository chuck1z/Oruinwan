Oruinwan is an all-in-one widget web application for your everyday needs.
Built with love from yours truly, your most-used widgets are just one click away!


Features:
1. The clock widget consists of a clock, a timer, and a stopwatch to help you track your time.
2. The calendar widget aids you in tracking your day.
3. The weather widget tells you your location's current temperature and climate.
4. The calculator widget consists of a simple calculator and a unit converter to assist your mathematical needs.
5. The notes widget helps you write down your thoughts and download it.
6. To move around the widgets, use the bottom menu to quickly switch widgets.

So, let's start optimizing your day with Oruinwan!
